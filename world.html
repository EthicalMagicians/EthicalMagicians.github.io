Our World

This demo expands the world developed in the story and art into a medium that explores time and 3D space, where objects and events have location and duration. Thematically the cycles of time are tuned into by the use of changing light colours evoking the seasonal passage of time, discoverable objects chosen to represent life stages, and through the use of time as a game mechanic.
